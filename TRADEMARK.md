# Threadbase Trademark Policy

Threadbase is the project name and brand of Ronen Mars.

The source code may be licensed under MIT, but the Threadbase name, logo, and branding are not included in that license.

---

## Allowed uses

You may use the Threadbase name to:

- refer to the original project,
- describe compatibility with Threadbase,
- link to official Threadbase repositories,
- discuss or review the project,
- contribute to the open-source project.

Examples:

- "This plugin integrates with Threadbase."
- "A fork of the Threadbase streamer."
- "Compatible with Threadbase Mobile."

---

## Restricted uses

You may not use the Threadbase name, logo, or branding to market derivative projects, hosted services, commercial products, or unrelated projects without permission.

Avoid names such as:

- "Threadbase Cloud"
- "Threadbase Pro"
- "Threadbase Enterprise"
- "Official Threadbase Hosting"
- "Threadbase Mobile Plus"

unless explicitly authorized.

---

## Forks and derivatives

You may fork the code under the applicable open-source license, but derivative projects should use a clearly distinct name and branding.

Please make it clear that your fork is not the official Threadbase project unless you have permission.
